🏗 About SiteSpy

SiteSpy is a mobile-first construction project records and estimation app built with Expo React Native, Firebase, and Firestore. It helps construction teams capture measurements, estimate material costs, organise site records, and review project history on any device — even in low-connectivity field conditions.

👤 My Contribution

My contribution to SiteSpy focuses on the mathematical accuracy and reliability of the cost estimation engine. I reviewed and validated calculations.js to ensure the formulas for material quantity estimation are correct, and worked on the cost constants used in estimateService.js to reflect realistic material pricing. My MATLAB coursework directly informed how I approached numerical validation and matrix-based quantity calculations that underpin the BOQ generation feature.

Files I worked on:

  • src/utils/calculations.js
  • src/services/estimateService.js
  • shared/cost-constants.js

💡 Reflection

My MATLAB training gave me a rigorous approach to numerical computation that was directly useful in validating SiteSpy's calculation engine. Small errors in construction cost estimates have real financial consequences, so precision matters. I learned to write test cases that verify formula outputs against known results, and to document the assumptions built into each calculation so future developers can maintain them confidently.
