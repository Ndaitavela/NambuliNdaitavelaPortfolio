About the Project

About SiteSpy

SiteSpy is a mobile-first construction project records and cost estimation app built with Expo React Native, Firebase, and Firestore. It helps construction teams capture measurements, estimate material costs, and review project history on any device — even in low-connectivity field conditions.

My Contribution

My Contribution

As a Metallurgical Engineering student, I brought a materials-science and process-engineering perspective to SiteSpy's cost estimation engine. I reviewed and validated calculations.js to ensure material quantity formulas are correct for construction contexts, and refined the cost constants in estimateService.js with awareness of how material properties affect pricing. My MATLAB training directly informed my approach to numerical validation and formula accuracy.
